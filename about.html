About The Modern Frame

Our History

Founded in 1995, The Modern Frame has been at the forefront of contemporary art for nearly three decades. Our gallery was established with a vision to bridge the gap between emerging artists and art enthusiasts, creating a space where innovation meets appreciation.

Located in the heart of the arts district, we have grown from a small exhibition space to a renowned gallery that showcases both established and emerging contemporary artists. Our commitment to artistic excellence and cultural dialogue has made us a cornerstone of the local art community.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see." - Edgar Degas

This philosophy guides our curatorial approach, as we strive to present artwork that challenges perspectives and ignites meaningful conversations about the world around us.
